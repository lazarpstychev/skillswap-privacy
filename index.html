Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SkillSwap. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using SkillSwap, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name and email address
  • Password (stored encrypted)
  • Profile photo and location (optional)

Usage Data

  • Skills you teach and want to learn
  • Messages and conversations
  • Session history and reviews
  • Likes and interactions with other users

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and language preference

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the SkillSwap service
  • To match you with suitable skill exchange partners
  • To enable communication between users
  • To display your profile to other users
  • To send notifications about matches and messages
  • To improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With other users — your profile, skills, and rating are visible to other SkillSwap users
  • With service providers — only as necessary to operate the service
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are encrypted using BCrypt
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS
  • Database access is restricted and monitored

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Edit or update your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

SkillSwap is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app. Your continued use of SkillSwap after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
